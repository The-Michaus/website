My Teaching Philosophy

I believe that every learner is an individual with different needs. It is essential for me to know my learners and to accommodate their learning styles and personalities.

I am a firm believer of positive reinforcement. Every learner strives for appraisal. I believe in my learners and my goal is to encourage them to be the best version of themselves.

My teaching approach is not only focused on the curriculum content but also to teach learners values, morals and life lessons.

My aim is to contribute towards raising and educating our children to become successful citizens of our country.
